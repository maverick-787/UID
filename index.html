🌿 Our Story

Where Tradition Meets
Modern Flavor

Saffron & Ember was born in 2018 from a simple belief — that restaurant-quality food should be accessible to everyone, delivered hot to your doorstep without compromise.

Our head chef, Meera Krishnan, trained across Rajasthan, Kerala and Italy, bringing a unique East-meets-West approach. Every dish is crafted fresh, using seasonal produce sourced within 100 km of our kitchen.
